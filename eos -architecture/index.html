About us

Our mission

We are an architecture studio with a twist! Our mission is to spread awareness about natural building through our work and build a community while we are at it.

Natural Building

Natural buildings, as exquisite works of art, seamlessly merge human ingenuity with the raw beauty of the environment. These architectural marvels, born from the ethos of sustainability and a deep respect for the Earth, embody a harmonious coexistence between man-made structures and the surrounding landscape.

Natural buildings often showcase innovative techniques that harness natural energies for heating, cooling, and lighting, fostering a connection with the cycles of the Earth. As functional art, they embody the principles of sustainability, embodying a timeless aesthetic that transcends fleeting trends.

Workshops

We have workshops for both beginners and advanced natural builders, in which we cover both theory and practical aspects of natural buildings. Each of our workshops are unique in terms of the content and design, and we constantly keep experimenting to teach you something new in each workshop!
