Studio eos

Vision

Studio is a place where mistakes are welcomed! We wanted to create a place where people can experiment and learn natural building. Studio is always a work in progress, and we keep adding elements to it all the time.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make this a place where we can ultimately promoted sustainable living. Apart from building shelters,we are also learning to grow food, and soon we would start work on ceramics and clothing

ashreya at yaksha forest

Vision

Yaksha is a monoculture farm with a vision to convert it into a permaculture food forest. Ashreya is our natural shelter at the farm, hand sculpted out of mud

For the foundation we have used Malba and all the materials are sourced locally. Mitti from the nearby community ground of the village. Sand from the nearby river. Stones from the nearest quary we could find. And straw from the village itself
