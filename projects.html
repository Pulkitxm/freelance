Studio Eos

Studio is a space where mistakes are welcomed, encouraging experimentation and learning in natural building. Constantly evolving, our studio hosts regular workshops, providing immersive learning experiences. Participants are invited to stay and create, fully engaging with the natural building process.

Ashreya - Yaksha Forest Garden

Yaksha is transforming from a monoculture farm into a permaculture food forest. Our natural shelter, Ashreya, is hand-sculpted from mud using the cob technique, with 80% of materials sourced locally. We also conduct natural building workshops, inviting many to experience and participate in the construction process.
